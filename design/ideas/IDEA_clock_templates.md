# Clock Templates

Hour templates defining song/jingle/break patterns for automated scheduling.

## Concept
Define repeating patterns like:
- 0:00 - Jingle
- 0:02 - Song (Pop)
- 0:05 - Song (Any)
- 0:08 - Commercial Break
- ...repeat
